Types of Anime


In anime there are different types of anime, Each of these types of anime are focused on a specific target of populatoin of viewers

These types of anime are:

Shonen:
It is one of the category anime made to suit young boys and theres often a main character who is normally a male that has muscles and good fighting techniques and some types of ability that improves their strengt and they would have character traits wich would be an appreciation for their friends and their willingness to fight for them.
Examples of this kind of anime would Son Goku from Dragon ball and Natsu Dragneel from Fairy tale

Seinen:
This type of anime targets young adults or young male views which would mean that it is similar to shonen anime however Seinen anime usually have the same stories and themes as shonen but there are more sophisticated than shonen anime which implies that there are more violent, sexual, satirocal or psychologically intense and therefore it carters to a more mature audince which finds its popular with high school teens

Shoujo:
It is the oppposite of shonen as shoujo is translated in english is young girl.Shoujo focuses more on interpersonal relationship and romance in the story compare to its opposite shonen which focuses on fighting and action while it focuses on dramatic atmospheres but also includes comedy and action as a result of this being Shoujo the protagonist of the story is mostly always a female character
